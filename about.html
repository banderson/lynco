About Lynco Fire Protection

Lynco Fire Protection Inc. is a full service Fire Sprinkler Contactor Founded in 1992, Lynco is a Leader in the Fire Sprinkler Industry serving Massachusetts and Southern New Hampshire, providing design, engineering, and installation services, repairs, testing, Inspections and 24hr Emergency service.

Our experienced and dedicated staff can provide design, installation and service of all your fire sprinkler needs for commercial, industrial, medical, educational and residential facilities including single family homes.

Expertise

Each key member of our design, installation and Inspection team has at least 18 years experience in their fields. We specialize in the design and retrofit installation of fire sprinkler systems in existing buildings.
